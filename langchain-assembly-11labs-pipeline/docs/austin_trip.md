## Key Info

- **Hotel:** Hotel Van Zandt — (512) 542-5300
- **Transport:** Uber/Lyft recommended. Parking downtown is expensive.
- **Weather:** Expect 85–95°F, humid. Bring sunscreen and light clothes.
- **Cash:** Some venues (Franklin BBQ) are cash only. Keep $40–$60 handy.
- **Music tip:** 6th Street is touristy. Prefer Red River Cultural District for local vibe.
